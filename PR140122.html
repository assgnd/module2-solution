Chicken

There are two popular method how to cook chicken. Hunan chicken vs Szechuan chicken. Hunan chicken use Doubanjiang, oyster sauce, chicken broth, soy sauce, rice wine, garlic, ginger, sugar, veggies, etc. Szechuan chicken use Szechuan peppercorn, dried red chilis, green onions, garlic, ginger, salt, sugar etc.

Beef

One of best beef taste is beef teriyaki. The teriyaki sauce consists of soy sauce, mirin, sesame oil, fish sauce, ginger, garlic and brown sugar. The sauce gets nice and thick with a simple cornstarch slurry (1 teaspoon cornstarch + 1 tablespoon cold water)
